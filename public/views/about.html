The number one ranked Keystone State Basketball Camps

The number one ranked Keystone State Camps are directed by Pete White, one of the most knowledgeable and respected coaches in Pennsylvania. Coach White has 17 District Championships in a row, a Pennsylvania record.

He also holds the record by winning 12 consecutive PIAA AAAA First Round Playoff Games. His 1984 undefeated 30-0 State Champions may be the best high school basketball team in Pennsylvania schoolboy history.

In 1995 he was named the Pennsylvania Big School Coach of the Year by the Associated Press and led his team to a state runner-up finish. Coach White has also been the State Basketball Director for the KEYSTONE STATE GAMES since its inception in 1983.

Jim Martin’s Scouting Report, The National Recruiter and The Pennsylvania Coaches’ Association ranked Keystone State #1 the best basketball camp in the East.
